About Me

I'm Olesia, a data enthusiast and aspiring developer with a strong curiosity for how things work and how data can make them better.

My journey into tech started with a deep interest in data — how to collect it, clean it, and use it to answer real-world questions. Through the Code the Dream courses, I built a solid foundation in programming, data pipelines, and web development, and discovered how much I enjoy turning messy problems into elegant solutions.

I'm comfortable working with Python, SQL, and JavaScript, and I've recently been building dashboards, scraping tools, and small web apps. I enjoy digging into projects that combine logic, creativity, and practical impact.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy exploring new tools, reading about data ethics and design, and hiking when the weather allows. My goal is to keep growing as a developer and contribute to projects that are useful, inclusive, and thoughtfully built.

I'm always open to learning, feedback, and collaboration.
